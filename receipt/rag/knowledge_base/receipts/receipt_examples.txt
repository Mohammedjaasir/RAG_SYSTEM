Receipt Field Patterns:

VENDOR INFORMATION:
- Usually at the top of receipt
- Company name in capital letters or large font
- Address on next 2-3 lines
- Phone number with "Tel:" or "Phone:" prefix
- VAT/Tax registration with "VAT REG:", "GSTIN:", "SST REG:" prefix

DATE AND TIME:
- Formats vary: DD/MM/YYYY, MM/DD/YYYY, DD-MMM-YYYY
- Time usually in HH:MM or HH:MM:SS format
- May appear as "Date:", "Tarikh:", or just the date value

INVOICE NUMBER:
- Look for "Invoice:", "Receipt:", "Bill:", "Check Number:", "Bil No:"
- Usually alphanumeric code
- May include year or sequence numbers

ITEMS:
- Quantity at start of line
- Item description in middle
- Price at end (may have @ for unit price)
- May span multiple lines for long descriptions

TOTALS:
- "SUBTOTAL" or "SUB TOTAL" before tax
- Tax lines with percentages (VAT, GST, CGST, SGST, SST)
- "TOTAL" or "AMOUNT TO PAY" as final amount
- May show "Net Total" for amount before tax

PAYMENT:
- Payment method: Cash, Card, VISA, MASTERCARD, AMEX
- Card numbers partially masked: XXXX or ****
- May include tip or gratuity
- Cash receipts show amount given and change

CURRENCY INDICATORS:
- £ for GBP (UK)
- € for EUR (Europe)
- $ for USD (US)
- ₹ for INR (India)
- RM for MYR (Malaysia)
